## Description

**TasteTogether** is a social food web app where food lovers come together to share recipes, discover new dishes, and enjoy meals socially even from a distance.

Users can:
- Share photos of homemade meals or restaurant experiences  
- Discover recipes and restaurants based on preferences  
- Join video rooms to cook, eat, or hang out together  
- Make food more social, interactive, and fun  

This repository contains the **updated frontend version**, focusing on improved UI, better user experience, and a modern React-based structure. The **full-stack implementation** and **live deployment** are linked above.
